Welcome aboard,

It's nice to have you here!

Before starting adding new ROMs into your library, please follow the next steps to enable the connectivity between RetroX and Google Drive.

After completion, a folder named 'Retrox' will be created in your Google Drive. This folder will be used to store all the games you add.

Note: RetroX will NOT have access to your personal files. The following steps will allow this app to have permissions limited to managing the 'RetroX' folder.

🚀

Let's start!

STEP 1

Log in into your Google Drive, then open https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/ and click the button + ENABLE APIS AND SERVICES.

STEP 2

Search the API Google Drive and press enter.

STEP 3

Select the Google Drive API on the list.

STEP 4

Click the ENABLE button.

STEP 5

Go to the OAuth consent screen section, select External and click the CREATE button.

STEP 6

Here you will need to enter some values:

  • App name: RetroX
  • User support email: Select your gmail address from the list
  • Authorized domains: Click + ADD DOMAIN and type retrox.app
  • Developer contact information: Type your gmail address

Finally click the button SAVE AND CONTINUE.

STEP 7

Click the button ADD OR REMOVE SCOPES.

STEP 8

Select .../auth/drive.file and then click the UPDATE button. Finally click SAVE AND CONTINUE.

STEP 9

Click the SAVE AND CONTINUE button.

STEP 10

Click the PUBLISH APP button.

STEP 11

Click CONFIRM.

STEP 12

At this point you should see Publishing Status: In production.

STEP 13

Go to the Credentials section, click + CREATE CREDENTIALS and select OAuth client ID.

STEP 14

Here you will need to enter some values:

  • Application type: Web application
  • Name: RetroX
  • Authorized Javascript origins: https://www.retrox.app
  • Authorized redirect URIs: https://www.retrox.app/callback

Finally click the button CREATE.

STEP 15

Copy the values from Client ID and Client secret.

⭐

Almost done!

Enter the values copied from the previous step and click the Save button.
